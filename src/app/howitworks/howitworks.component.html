How it works
  • 1
    Invest

    Invest from as little as GHS 1 into our Education Fund for your own education or to support someone in need.

  • 2
    The Crowd Invests

    Thousands of other investors just like yourself also invest in the same fund.

  • 3
    Monthly Draw

    At the end of the month a blind draw approved by the Gaming Commission is made to randomly select our monthly cash winners.

  • 4
    Potential Returns

    Winning investors will receive 1,000% of their invested amounts into the fund

  • 5
    Didn’t win? Don’t worry

    If you did not win for the month, don’t worry. We hold a secondary draw every month where we give out smart phones, laptops , etc.

  • 6
    Investment Frequency

    You can invest as many times and as often as you want to increase your chances of winning.
